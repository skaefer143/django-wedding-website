Marissa Tailleur - Maid of Honor

Marissa is definitely as sweet and gentle as she first comes across - but I have the privilege of also knowing her to be a fierce friend, loyal in every situation, and as stubborn as they come. It's pretty rare to find someone that loves everyone they meet and never has a bad word to say about anyone, but one of these rare souls is my sister and one of my best friends. Her art is amazing, and Asian soups divine - if you're ever going anywhere in Asia, bring Marissa. Also don't borrow her erasers (even if you return it she will notice it's out of place hahaha).


Best memories include:

  • Her role as "Luke Walker" in Car Wars (the environmental Star Wars)
  • Streaking around the house together as children when guests were over
  • Playing Barbies for endless hours
  • Backpacking in Tonquin and Thunderwater
